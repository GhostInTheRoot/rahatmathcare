A Passion for Teaching Mathematics

Nur Alam Rahat founded Rahat Math Care in 2023 with a single mission: to give students in Gazipur access to the highest quality mathematics education and help them fulfill their engineering ambitions.

Graduating from BBAGC with a degree in Pure Physics, he brings both rigorous academic training and real-world engineering perspective into every lesson. His approach combines conceptual clarity with intensive exam practice.

Over more than a decade of teaching, he has refined a unique methodology that breaks complex topics into understandable steps — building genuine confidence alongside knowledge.
